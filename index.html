About YatraVandi

Travel Smarter with YatraVandi

YatraVandi, powered by Tech Venue, is a modern bus booking platform built to make travel simple, secure and convenient. Whether you're a daily commuter, student, business traveller, family or tourist, we help you discover the best bus options, compare fares and book tickets within seconds.

Our mission is to deliver a seamless digital travel experience through instant ticket booking, secure online payments, real-time seat selection and dedicated customer support. With exciting upcoming features like WhatsApp Booking, AI Voice Booking and Live Bus Tracking, YatraVandi is redefining the future of bus travel.
